Tilžės turgus

Tilžės g. 51 Klaipėda; +370 46 313552

For the past four yars I have manage my budget and my diet. I have learned how to safe money but still buy quality products for my meals.

Back home, we used to buy groceries on the market because it is cheaper and more fresh than in stores.

In Klaipeda I prefer to shop in Maxima or Lidl. However, this summer I found a great place with fresh fruit and veggies and I have to share it with EVERYONE!

Have you ever bought delicious and ripe avocado for 5 cents? I have!

This place has great deals every day. On the third image, you can see what product I usually buy there. That day I spent no more than 3 EUR.

You have to go there!

Picnic with friends

I guess, everyone had great plans for the summer as I did. However, ue to the pandemic, I had a chance to stay in Klaipeda. I accepted the circumstances and decided to enjoy summer in Lithuania.

Klaipeda is the best place for picnics at the beach. Only if the weather is good of course :D

Organizing a picnic with close friends in a park, beach or forest is the best decision when all the places are closed.

I prefer to take sandwiches, pancakes, crepes, cookies, fruit, berries, and tea or coffee.

You do not need to order pizza or kebabas. You can take some healthy, cheap, and delicious snacks and spend a great time with your friends.
